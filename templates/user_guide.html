Your resume template is the master file the AI draws from when customising your CV for each job. You upload it once and it stays on your profile.

What you need to upload
  • A .docx (Word) file — PDFs are not supported
  • Must contain a HEADLINE VARIATIONS section and an EXPERIENCE BULLETS section using those exact headings
  • Each bullet must be on its own line, at least 80 characters long
  • Optionally group bullets under category sub-headings (e.g. Risk Management, Leadership) to improve AI selection accuracy
How to update it

Go to Resume in the top nav → upload a new file at any time to replace the existing one. The page also shows the required format so you always know what's expected.

The AI prompt controls how the AI selects and scores your CV bullets. It runs every time you click Customise CV for this role.

Default template vs custom prompt
  • Default template: A pre-written prompt that works well for most finance roles. No setup needed.
  • Custom prompt: Personalise the AI instructions. Use the Guided Builder — answer a few quick questions and it writes the prompt for you automatically.
How to use the Guided Builder

Go to AI Prompt → fill in the target role, seniority, industry, and strengths → click Generate My Prompt → review the result → click Save Custom Prompt.

💡 If you're new, start with the default prompt. Only customise once you've run a few analyses and know what you'd like to change.

This is the core feature. Paste any job description and the AI compares it against your resume bullets to find the best fit.

Step by step
  1. Go to the AI Match Tool tab
  2. Paste the full job description into the text box
  3. Click Analyse Job — this usually takes 10–30 seconds
  4. Review your match score and the recommended bullets
  5. Click Customise CV for this role when ready
What the score means
  • 80–100% — Strong match, high chance of shortlist
  • 60–79% — Good match, worth applying
  • Below 60% — Weaker overlap; consider whether to apply
💡 Paste the entire job posting, not just the requirements section. The more context the AI has, the better its analysis.

After running a job analysis, this step lets you review and tweak the AI's bullet selection before generating your tailored CV.

What you can do here
  • See which bullets the AI selected and why
  • Tick or untick individual bullets to include or exclude them
  • Choose your preferred headline variation
  • Click Generate CV to create the tailored document
Downloading your CV

After generating, you'll land on the CV Preview page where you can review the final layout and download the .docx file.

After customising your CV, you land here automatically. This page shows the final assembled document before you download it.

What you'll see
  • Your selected headline at the top
  • All chosen bullets in the order they'll appear in the document
  • A Download .docx button to save the final file
✅ The downloaded file matches the formatting of your original template — just open in Word and it's ready to send.

Got an interview? This feature generates tailored practice questions based on the job description and your resume bullets.

How to use it
  1. Go to the Interview Prep tab
  2. Select a role you've already customised your CV for
  3. Work through 8 AI-generated questions one at a time
  4. Submit each answer for instant AI feedback
  5. Review your overall performance summary at the end
What you get
  • Role-specific competency and behavioural questions
  • AI feedback on each answer you submit
  • A score and summary at the end of the session
